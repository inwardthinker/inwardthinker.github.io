The Builder's Journey

IIT Bombay CS '15. Started in big data across finance, healthcare, and supply chain. Moved to Copenhagen in 2018, spent three years building enterprise ETL at Tradeshift.

Went all-in on Web3 in 2021 — not just as an engineer, but as a user. Traded DeFi, collected NFTs, bet on prediction markets with my own money. That first-hand consumer experience became my edge.

Now I'm CTO & Cofounder at Barter, where I've shipped a crypto sportsbook and prediction markets platform from zero. I operate across engineering, product, and strategy — and still ship features end-to-end independently.
